Materials
Tinkercad
Goal
- wires
- breadboard
- servo
- ultrasonic sensor
- arduino uno
Whenever I play piano, I want the light to turn on by itself. I have an ultrasonic sensor detect me walk past, which then calls the servo to activate, flicking on the lever of the light. When I leave, the ultrasonic sensor makes the servo turn off the lights.
